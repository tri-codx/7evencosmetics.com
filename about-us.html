Our Story

7even is an International beauty brand that blends New Zealand’s advanced skincare science with India’s rich beauty heritage.

Our products are specially formulated for Indian skin tones and climate, delivering high performance with the goodness of natural and botanical ingredients.

At 7even Cosmetics, we focus on innovation, inclusivity, and quality. Our makeup range — from foundations to lipsticks — is gentle on the skin, long-lasting, and crafted to enhance natural beauty while solving common Indian skin concerns such as pigmentation, oiliness, and pollution sensitivity.

By combining New Zealand formulations with India’s strong manufacturing expertise, 7even is committed to creating clean, effective, and sustainable beauty products.

Just like the seven colors of the rainbow, our range represents purity, inclusivity, and the celebration of individuality — reflecting the vibrant spectrum of real Indian beauty.

Mission

Our mission is to deliver high-performance, skin-friendly beauty products crafted with natural and botanical ingredients. We aim to solve common Indian skin concerns, celebrate diversity, and promote self-expression through innovative formulations, sustainable practices, and world-class manufacturing.

By combining global innovation with local relevance, we strive to offer beauty solutions that truly work for every Indian consumer.

Vision

To become India’s most trusted beauty brand by blending New Zealand’s advanced skincare science with India’s diverse beauty needs — creating clean, effective, and inclusive cosmetics that empower every individual to embrace their natural beauty.
